Krishnakumar "KK" Kunnath widely considered as one of the best singers and composers of this generation, sent shockwaves across the nation when his the news of his death reached people.

KK brought joy and jubilation to one's life with his amazing voice along with his unique ability to portray emotions while singing.

"Don't cry because it's over. Smile because it happened." KK will always be remembered in our hearts and his tunes immortalized...
